Waypoint — Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

Your data belongs to you.

What we collect

Nothing. Your panic attack history, trigger logs, calm day count, and notes are stored exclusively on your device. We have no servers. We have no accounts. We never see your data.

What leaves your device

Nothing. Waypoint does not transmit any data over the internet. There are no analytics, no crash reporting, no third-party SDKs, and no advertising networks inside this app.

Notifications

If you enable daily check-ins, iOS schedules them locally on your device. No data is sent anywhere to make this work.

Exporting your data

When you use the in-app export feature, you control where it goes. We never receive a copy.

Deleting your data

Use "Delete all data" in Settings, or delete the app. Either action permanently removes everything.

Contact

Questions about this policy? Contact us at hello@waypointreset.com
