# Privacy Policy — Waypoint: Panic Never Wins

Last updated: June 25, 2026

## No Data Collection

Waypoint does not collect, transmit, or share any personal data. Everything you record in the app stays on your device.

## What Stays on Your Device

- Panic attack logs
- Calm day streaks
- Weekly review data

## No Accounts, No Cloud, No Tracking

Waypoint requires no account to use. No data is sent to any server. No analytics or tracking of any kind is used.

## Third Parties

Waypoint uses no third-party SDKs, advertising networks, or analytics tools.
